Aakash Sharma is a researcher and PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at UiT - The Arctic University of Norway. His research focuses on distributed systems, data analytics, and privacy-preserving technologies. He has also served as a board member of TODOS, a support organization for PhD candidates at UiT. He is associated with the Cyber Security Group and Health Data Lab.

Although he does not maintain personal social media accounts, he manages the official Twitter and YouTube accounts of ACM SIGOPS.

Aakash started Lohpi, as his PhD research project. To learn more, check his publications, CV, or NVA profile.

Aakash holds an MSc in Distributed Software Systems (2017) from the Department of Computer Science at TU Darmstadt, Germany. He currently lives in Tromsø, Norway.

Aakash is a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science at UiT - The Arctic University of Norway. His research interests include distributed systems, data analytics and privacy-preserving technologies. He served as a board member of TODOS. In the absence of any personal social media account, he manages ACM SIGOPS' official Twitter and YouTube accounts.

He holds a MSc in Distributed Software Systems (2017) from the Department of Computer Science at TU Darmstadt, Germany. Prior to his Master's degree, he worked in various roles at Media Lab Asia, IBM India Software Labs and Persistent Systems. He currently lives in Senja.
